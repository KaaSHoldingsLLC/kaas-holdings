# Module 3: Product Portfolio Specifications

KaaS Holdings LLC maintains 12 core proprietary systems, structured across four operational domains:

---

## 1. Legal & Regulatory Infrastructure

### ⚖️ Co-Executor (AI Estate Settlement)
- **Objective:** Demystify and automate the probate and estate administration process.
- **Key Mechanics:** Deploys a Gemini-powered procedural engine in Google AI Studio. Translates dense court rules (such as the Arizona Rules of Civil Procedure) into guided, step-by-step document compilation workflows. 
- **Current Assets:** System logic history, drafted SAFE Founder Agreements, procedural mapping frameworks.

### 📜 FinLex (Regulatory Compliance Notification)
- **Objective:** Turn dense regulatory updates into actionable intelligence.
- **Key Mechanics:** Autonomous scraper and parsing agent live on Replit. Processes SEC, IRS, and local compliance changes, translates them into plain English compliance briefs, and triggers automatic notification alerts.
- **Current Assets:** Live publishing history, active system prompts, marketing/pricing tier structures.

### 📝 Secured Ledger (The Standing Protocol)
- **Objective:** Automate the assertion of legal capacity and standing in commercial disputes.
- **Key Mechanics:** A modular logic engine consisting of a UCC Analysis Module, a Statutory Response Generator, and an automated court deadline tracker.

### 🏛️ Sovereign Probate & Estate Templates
- **Objective:** Provide direct-access legal standing kits for non-attorney administrators.
- **Key Mechanics:** Pre-configured legal document sets, structural interrogatories, and master filings mapped to state-specific probate codes.

## 3. Privacy & Physical Sovereignty

### 📡 Project DNG-SBS (Privacy-First Spatial RF Sensing)
- **Objective:** Eliminate camera-free surveillance vulnerabilities in the newly ratified IEEE 802.11bf Wi-Fi standard.
- **Key Mechanics:** Compact, double-negative (DNG) passive metamaterial structures placed over standard antenna apertures. Uses sub-wavelength negative refraction to focus 3.3-5.7 GHz signals to 0.25λ. Local edge processors execute differential-delta calculations to output binary occupancy alerts without generating or storing high-density biometric streams.
- **Current Assets:** Complete market landscape analysis, academic alignment briefs, and cryptographic inception timestamp.

### 🆔 Identity-Locator
- **Objective:** Assert absolute ownership over personal identity records.
- **Key Mechanics:** Published application tracking public and private database endpoints to locate where living soul identity data is being stored, traded, or misappropriated.
- **Current Assets:** Live deployment build and identity verification schema.
